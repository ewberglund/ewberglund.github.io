FAQ

When should I arrive?
The ceremony starts at 5pm on May 1st, please arrive at least 30 minutes before to allow for time to get settled. There will be a welcome event the evening of April 30th for everyone who is around.
When should I RSVP by?
Please submit your RSVP by March 13th, and reserve a room by March 20th to use the discount. Check the RSVP section of our website for more info! :)
Where should I fly into?
San Francisco(SFO) is the most convenient airport to Half Moon Bay and only 19 miles away. San Jose(SJC) and Oakland(OAK) are also solid options, both around 40 miles away.
What's the dress code?
For the wedding, Semi-formal. Keep in mind that weather in Half Moon Bay in May can be highly variable, with average temperatures ranging between 44 and 60, and can be windy. The ceremony will be outdoors and on grass, with the reception inside. For the welcome dinner attire is business casual and will be indoors, and the next morning brunch is casual and will be on a semi-covered patio.
Is there parking at the venue?
Yes, there is ample free parking at the hotel.
Where should I stay?
Oceano is the best option, easily walkable to Thursday and Friday's events. We have a room block side aside with a group rate, just mention that you're booking for the "de Vera-Berglund Wedding". Be sure to book your room by March 20th.
Are kids welcome?
Yes! All ages are welcome.
Do you have a registry?
No, please no gifts, we have limited space for new items, just come and enjoy!
What are your honeymoon plans?
We'll be taking a short trip after the wedding, and then planning for a larger trip later this year that is still in the works.
